Who We Are

Built for Sierra Leone.
Powered by Community.

Salone HealthConnect is a digital healthcare directory and advisory platform dedicated to bridging the gap between patients and medical services across all 14 districts of Sierra Leone.

Our Mission

To make healthcare information accessible, accurate, and actionable for every person in Sierra Leone — whether they are in Freetown or a remote village in Kono — by providing a centralized, low-data-capable platform for finding facilities, booking doctors, and coordinating care.

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where no life is lost because someone could not find the nearest hospital, did not know which doctor to see, or could not reach a blood donor in time. We envision a fully connected national health ecosystem, driven by community participation and verified data.

Our Values

  • Accessibility — low-data design for every Sierra Leonean
  • Accuracy — verified, up-to-date facility information
  • Community — built with and for local people
  • Transparency — open about our data sources and limitations
  • Equity — equal focus on urban and rural health access

The Problem

Healthcare access in Sierra Leone is fragmented

Sierra Leone has made significant strides in expanding healthcare infrastructure, yet millions of people still struggle to find basic health information when they need it most. In an emergency, locating the nearest open hospital, contacting the right specialist, or finding an available blood donor can take hours — time that costs lives.

  • Fragmented information

    Facility contact details, opening hours, and available services are scattered across paper directories, word of mouth, and outdated websites.

  • Blood shortage coordination

    Blood banks often run critically low with no public-facing system to alert donors or match urgent requests.

  • Rural-urban divide

    Patients in Bo, Kenema, Kono, and other districts have limited visibility into what specialist care is available and where.

  • High data costs

    Existing digital health tools are designed for high-bandwidth environments, making them inaccessible for most Sierra Leoneans.

What We Offer

A complete healthcare hub

Everything a patient, caregiver, or health worker needs — in one place.

🏥

Hospital Directory

A searchable, map-integrated directory of government, private, and NGO-run health facilities across all districts. Each listing includes open hours, services offered, contact details, and real-time availability status.

📅

Doctor Appointments

Browse verified doctors by specialty and hospital. View their qualifications, available days, and book time slots directly — reducing the need to travel for information before receiving care.

🩸

Blood Donation Network

A live blood bank status system showing stock levels by type at major facilities, with a donor registration portal to help connect patients with compatible donors quickly.

💊

Medical Supply Donations

A donation coordination platform for medicines, equipment, and financial contributions — matching donors with facilities in greatest need across the country.

🚨

Emergency Information

Prominently displayed emergency contacts, ambulance numbers, and nearest facility routing to reduce life-threatening delays during medical emergencies.

📲

Low-Data Design

The platform is built with data efficiency as a core requirement — not an afterthought — so it works reliably on 2G connections and low-end smartphones.
